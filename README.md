# Emergency Tree Board

A standalone public-information project for storm-related tree hazards, preparedness guidance, operational updates, and community reporting across Mobile and the central Gulf Coast.

## Project boundaries

This repository is intentionally separate from:

- `tree251.xyz`, The Arborist business website
- Mobile Tree Intelligence, the permit and public-records platform
- Municipal 311 systems, emergency management agencies, utilities, and government dashboards

The Emergency Tree Board is an independent educational and situational-awareness project. It is not an emergency dispatch service and is not affiliated with or endorsed by a municipality, government agency, utility, nonprofit, or tree board.

## Initial product goals

- Publish clear tree-related storm readiness guidance
- Display current operating status and verified updates
- Explain what residents should report, document, and avoid
- Provide a future pathway for community-submitted tree hazard reports
- Distinguish urgent life-safety emergencies from non-emergency tree concerns
- Preserve privacy by avoiding public display of exact residential addresses

## Safety standard

For immediate danger to life, active utility contact, fire, blocked emergency access, or a tree on an occupied structure, users should contact the appropriate emergency service or utility. This project does not replace 911, utility emergency lines, municipal reporting systems, or an on-site arborist assessment.
